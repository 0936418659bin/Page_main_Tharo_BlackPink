BLACK PINK

We love music

Debuting in August 2016, with a series of music products that "crazy" the charts and broke records, BLACKPINK quickly became one of the top girl groups in Kpop and had impressive success. In the world. In particular, the 4 members of the group, each member has a high level of recognition and is loved by many domestic and international fans. The name Blackpink reflects the image that the group pursues, the style and personality of the 4 members are similar to the group's name, pink and black. Black represents personality and strength through two members Jennie and Lisa, and pink represents gentleness and femininity through Rose and Jisoo, the four members that make up Blackpink. In addition, the group's name also has a meaning that, according to YG's representative, is to refute the popular perception of the color pink. Pink is often used to express beauty, but Blackpink means "Beauty is not everything."
